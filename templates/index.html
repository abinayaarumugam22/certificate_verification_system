Process

How It Works

01
Institution Registers Certificate

The institution uploads the original certificate image along with student details. The system generates SHA-256 and perceptual hashes and stores them securely in the database.

02
Certificate Is Issued to Student

The student receives the certificate. They can log in to view their registration status and confirm their certificate is on record.

03
Verifier Checks Authenticity

Any verifier — employer, university, or official — uploads the certificate and enters the register number. The system compares hashes and returns VALID, POSSIBLY VALID, or TAMPERED status instantly.

04
Tampered Regions Highlighted

If tampering is detected, the system uses SSIM analysis to identify and visually highlight the exact regions that were modified, providing clear evidence.
